Welcome to Vietnam

Vietnam, a charming S-shaped country, stands out with its beautiful natural landscapes and rich culture. From majestic Ha Long Bay, poetic Sapa terraced fields, to vibrant Ho Chi Minh City and ancient Hanoi, each region has its own unique identity. This country is famous for its diverse and attractive dishes such as pho, banh mi and spring rolls. The heroic history and hospitable and friendly people are also highlights that create a strong attraction for Vietnam, attracting tourists from all over the world.
